About

Behind the lens & the screen

I'm Alex — a visual artist based between NYC and LA. With over 8 years of experience across photography, post-production, and design, I help brands and individuals tell their stories with intention and edge.

My work blends documentary honesty with cinematic polish. Whether it's a campaign, a music video, or a brand identity, I bring the same level of care and obsession to every frame and pixel.

Clients include: Nike, Vogue, Adidas, and independent musicians worldwide.
